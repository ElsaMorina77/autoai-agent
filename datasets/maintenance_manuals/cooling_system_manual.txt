Cooling System Diagnostic Manual

Relevant systems:
- Radiator
- Coolant reservoir
- Water pump
- Thermostat
- Cooling fan
- Coolant hoses

Common symptoms:
- Engine overheating
- Rising temperature gauge
- Coolant leak under vehicle
- Burning smell
- Engine warning light

Diagnostic procedure:
1. Inspect coolant reservoir level.
2. Check for visible leaks around hoses, radiator, and water pump.
3. Pressure-test the cooling system.
4. Verify thermostat opens at operating temperature.
5. Inspect radiator airflow and cooling fan operation.

Risk note:
Continued driving during overheating can cause cylinder head gasket damage, engine warping, or complete engine failure.
