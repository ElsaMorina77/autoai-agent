Brake System Diagnostic Manual

Relevant systems:
- Brake pads
- Brake rotors
- Brake calipers
- Brake fluid
- ABS sensors

Common symptoms:
- Brake noise
- Soft brake pedal
- ABS warning light
- Vibration during braking
- Reduced braking performance

Diagnostic procedure:
1. Inspect brake pad thickness.
2. Inspect rotor surface and runout.
3. Check brake fluid level and contamination.
4. Scan ABS module for fault codes.
5. Verify brake hydraulic pressure.

Risk note:
Brake faults are safety-critical and should be inspected before the vehicle continues normal operation.
