Battery and Charging System Manual

Relevant systems:
- Battery
- Alternator
- Starter motor
- Ground wiring
- Battery terminals

Common symptoms:
- Hard start
- Flickering dashboard lights
- Low voltage
- Battery warning light
- Vehicle starts after jump start

Diagnostic procedure:
1. Measure resting battery voltage.
2. Perform battery load test.
3. Test alternator charging voltage.
4. Inspect battery terminals for corrosion.
5. Check ground connections and parasitic draw.

Risk note:
A weak battery or failing alternator can cause unexpected no-start situations and electronic system instability.
